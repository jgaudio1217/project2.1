Historical Data
Segregation - Seperating people based on race or skin color (School, Work, Military, etc.)
Racial Discrimination - Discriminating against someone based on race or skin color
When Segragation was abolished some black children had to be escorted by police because the risk of being harmed was higher then having a safe day at school.
Racial Discrimination made it impossible for people of color to get stable jobs and lead normal lives. This forced them to buy low income homes in poor neighborhoods.
